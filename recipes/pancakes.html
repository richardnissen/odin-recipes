Recipe for Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe for pancakes

I prefer to make mine vegan

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plant milk of choice
  • 2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ¼ cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon maple syrup

Steps

  1. First, add your 3 wet ingredients in a bowl and stir to combine. The vinegar will curdle the milk slightly, giving you a quick "buttermilk."
  2. In a seperate bowl, add your 3 dry ingredeints and stir to combine. This step is important as it let's you evenly incorporate the baking powder into the flour.
  3. Add your wet ingredients to your dry ingredients and whisk to combine. Let rest 3 to 5 minutes. While you preheat a nonstick skillet over medium.
  4. When the skillet is warm, add 3 x 4in circles of pancake batter to the skillet. (If you use a ¼ measuring cup to transfer the batter to the skillet, this is a little less than a full ¼ cup.)
  5. Let the pancakes cook until they begin to bubble and the edges start to crisp. When the underside has turned a golden brown color, flip over and cook on the other side. After the pancakes are done, transfer to a plate or a sheet tray in the oven and repeat the process until all of the pancakes are done. Serve and enjoy!
